# Explanation

Prompt engineering is the strategic art of refining input queries to ensure AI models deliver high-quality, precise, and contextually relevant results.

Utilizing specific prompting methodologies improves:
* The accuracy of technical responses.
* Data visualization and structural hierarchy.
* Depth of information and context.
* Simplification of complex scientific topics.

This experiment applies Unit 2 prompting strategies to a Unit 5 domain (Renewable Energy).

# Selected Use Case (Unit 5)

## Renewable Energy Systems
Renewable energy is power generated from natural processes that are replenished at a rate equal to or faster than they are consumed. Examples include solar, wind, and geothermal energy. These systems are critical for reducing carbon footprints and ensuring environmental sustainability.

# 1. Straightforward Prompts

## Definition
A straightforward prompt is a direct, concise instruction that tells the AI exactly what to do without providing unnecessary background details.

---

### Prompt (ChatGPT):
Define the term "Photovoltaic effect" in two sentences.

**Output:**
The photovoltaic effect is the process by which a solar cell converts sunlight directly into electricity. When light strikes the semiconductor material, it excites electrons, creating an electric current.

---

### Prompt (Gemini):
List three primary components of a wind turbine.

**Output:**
* Rotor blades
* Nacelle
* Tower

---

# 2. Tabular Format Prompting

## Definition
Tabular prompting instructs the AI to organize its response into a table, making comparisons and data analysis much easier to read.

---

### Prompt (ChatGPT):
Create a table comparing Solar Energy and Wind Energy based on source, availability, and main advantage.

**Output:**

| Feature | Solar Energy | Wind Energy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Source** | Sunlight | Air Currents |
| **Availability** | Daytime only | Intermittent (Day/Night) |
| **Main Advantage** | Low maintenance | High efficiency in right areas |

---

### Prompt (Gemini):
Generate a table of 4 renewable energy types and their typical efficiency percentages.

**Output:**

| Energy Type | Average Efficiency |
| :--- | :--- |
| Solar PV | 15% - 22% |
| Wind | 30% - 50% |
| Hydroelectric | 90% |
| Geothermal | 10% - 15% |

---

# 3. Preceding Question Prompting

## Definition
This technique involves asking an initial question to establish context, followed by a deeper question that builds upon the first answer.

---

### Prompt (ChatGPT):
What are fossil fuels? Following that, explain why shifting to renewable energy is necessary to stop the greenhouse effect.

**Output:**
Fossil fuels are natural fuels like coal and oil formed from ancient organic matter. Shifting to renewables is necessary because fossil fuels release massive amounts of CO2, whereas renewables produce little to no emissions, preventing further heat-trapping in the atmosphere.

---

# 4. Missing Word Prompting

## Definition
Also known as "fill-in-the-blank," this method tests the AI's ability to complete a statement accurately using specific terminology.

---

### Prompt (Gemini):
The process of capturing heat from within the Earth is known as ______ energy.

**Output:**
Geothermal

---

### Prompt (Gemini):
A ______ is used to convert DC power from solar panels into AC power for home use.

**Output:**
Inverter

# Evaluation Method

## Method Used: Qualitative Performance Assessment
The outputs were graded based on four key metrics:
* **Clarity:** Was the language easy to interpret?
* **Accuracy:** Were the technical facts correct?
* **Relevance:** Did the AI follow the specific formatting constraints?
* **Completeness:** Did the response address all parts of the prompt?

# Conclusion
